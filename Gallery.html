Flowers carry a lot of importance in our lives. Ancient literature in India is replete with names of various flowers. Flowers are considered auspicious and they are also an integral part of Hindu worshipping. In India, no worship of God that is considered complete without adding colourful, vibrant and fragrant flowers. But it is not just any flower that is offered to any deity, rather, each flower holds a different significance and thus chosen to be offered to a particular deity. This is what makes them sacred today and gives them a religious appeal. Devotees make a garland of flowers to dedicate it to God.
